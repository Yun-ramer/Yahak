WEB

The World Wide Web (WWW), commonly known as the Web , is an information system where documents and other resources are identified by Uniform Resource Locators(URLs, such as https://www.example.com/). which may be interlinked by hypertext, and are accessible over the Internet. The resource of the WWW may be accessed by users by of software application called a seb browser.
